About me:

For the past decade, I have devoted myself to providing the highest quality speech and language services to children who present with a variety of communication disorders, including:

  • Articulation and phonological disorders
  • Auditory processing disorders
  • Autistic spectrum disorders
  • Apraxia
  • Language processing disorders
  • Oral-motor weaknesses
  • Social-pragmatic disorders
  • Specific language impairment
  • Stuttering and related fluency disorders
  • Voice disorders
  • Word retrieval deficits

My mission is simple:

Via ongoing assessment, family/caregiver participation and ever-evolving, individualized therapy, I take great pride in helping my clients become empowered, informed and skillful communicators.

I have worked in educational, clinical, and home-based settings with children ranging in age from preschool through high school. Evaluations and therapy programs are available in home or office upon request.

Let's talk:

Whether your child would benefit from a naturalistic, play-based approach, or needs a more structured environment, you can rest assured that I will construct a nurturing, inspiring and enriching program to address your child’s individual needs.

I look forward to helping you as best I can.
