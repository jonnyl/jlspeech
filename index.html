Services

For the past decade, I have devoted myself to providing the highest quality of speech and language services to children with communication disorders via ongoing assessment, family involvement and ever-evolving treatment programs, carefully catered to address my clients’ unique needs and learning styles. As a speech language pathologist, I have worked extensively with youngsters who present with various language and articulation disorders, auditory processing disorders, receptive and expressive language delays, writing disorders, and executive function deficits. Collaboration with family members, teachers and other interdisciplinary team members has aided me in fostering a safe, supportive learning environment for each of my clients.

Whether your child would benefit from a naturalistic, play-based approach, or needs a more structured environment, you can rest assured that I will create a nurturing, motivating and enriching program to address your child’s individual needs.

I have worked in educational, clinical, and home-based settings with children ranging in age from preschool through high school. Evaluations and therapy programs are available in home or office upon request.

Diagnostic and therapy services are available both individually and [when appropriate] in small groups to children and adolescents with a range of communication and learning difficulties.

About Jackie.

My name is Jackie Levine and I am a NJ (& NY) State licensed and certified speech-language pathologist new to the town of South Orange. In an effort to continue my life’s work more locally, I am offering in-home speech-language therapy programs to South Orange and it’s neighboring communities.
