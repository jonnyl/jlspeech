About me

For the past decade, I have devoted myself to providing the highest quality of speech and language services to children who present with a variety of communication disorders. Areas of specialty include:

  • Articulation and phonological disorders
  • Auditory processing disorders
  • Autistic spectrum disorders
  • Childhood apraxia of speech
  • Oral-motor weakness
  • Social-pragmatic disorders
  • Specific language impairment
  • Stuttering and related fluency disorders

My mission is simple

Via ongoing assessment, family/caregiver partnership, and ever-evolving, individualized therapy planning, I strive to help my clients become empowered, informed and skillful communicators. I take great pride in my ability to engage children in an enjoyable learning process, teach effective strategies which lead to communicative success, and have fun while doing so!

I have worked in educational, clinical, and home-based settings with children ranging in age from toddler through teen. Evaluations and therapy programs are available in home or office, upon request.
